Release Order

This is the order in which the Monogatari series was originally released to the public. It's recommended for new viewers as it preserves the intended mystery of the narrative, gradually unveiling the story's secrets. Watching in this order replicates the original experience of the audience as the series unfolded.

Bakemonogatari

15 Episodes | 2009 - 2010

A supernatural tale of high school student Koyomi Araragi, whose encounters with oddities change his life forever.

"Monsters aren’t born—they’re made."

Kizumonogatari

3 Movies | 2016 - 2017

The origin story showing Araragi's transformation into a half-vampire and the events leading to Bakemonogatari.

"Pain can be forgotten. But the scars remain."

Nisemonogatari

11 Episodes | 2012

Explores the theme of authenticity and deception, focusing on Araragi’s sisters and their supernatural troubles.

"The truth is often stranger than fiction."

Nekomonogatari: Kuro

4 Episodes | 2012

Delves into the backstory of Tsubasa Hanekawa and her encounter with the “Black Hanekawa” phenomenon.

"When the darkness rises, so do we."

Monogatari Series: Second Season

26 Episodes | 2013

Continues the supernatural adventures and deepens character arcs with new mysteries and revelations.

"Every story has its shadows."

Hanamonogatari

5 Episodes | 2014

Focuses on Suruga Kanbaru’s story and her struggles with a mysterious monkey’s paw.

"Desire is a double-edged sword."

Tsukimonogatari

4 Episodes | 2014

Centers on Yotsugi Ononoki and her unique perspective in the continuing supernatural saga.

"Life is a story we tell ourselves."

Owarimonogatari (First Season)

12 Episodes | 2015

Unveils deeper layers of Araragi's past and his encounters with various oddities.

"The end is just a new beginning."

Koyomimonogatari

12 Episodes | 2016

Short stories focusing on Koyomi Araragi's life between major events.

"Every moment is a thread in the tapestry."

Owarimonogatari (Second Season)

7 Episodes | 2017

Continues resolving mysteries surrounding Araragi's journey.

"Facing truth is the hardest battle."

Zoku Owarimonogatari

6 Episodes | 2018

An epilogue revealing new twists and the fate of the characters.

"Time changes everything, but memories endure."
